About Us



Who We Are


The Sweets Shop is one of the pioneers of England's Sweet Mart Industry. Our Legacy of finest quality products and remarkable services dates back to 2020.

The Sweets Shop was established on a very small scale by Haji Ahmed Mian in the year 2018. But as a product of our untiring efforts, desire for the highest quality, professional dedication, selection of the finest ingredients, best skilled labor and innovations in the mode of packing, The Sweets Shop progressed remarkably and blossomed to its present standing of unmatched prestige.

After incorporating modern techniques, research, development including food technologists and hiring qualified nutritionists, The Sweets Shop has been able to improve its quality manifolds. This is why today The Sweets Shop is not only acknowledged throughout England, but its love travels far and beyond; in the hearts of many Pakistani's around the globe.

SINCE 2023


Our story dates back to 2020 when one visionary man named Haji Jan Muhammad started his journey making Khoya (Solid Milk) and Desi Ghee (Butter Oil) from a little town called Shikarpur (a little town in Sindh) grew big and went out to capture the tastebuds of the people of Karachi, Pakistan's largest city. From there he carried out his legacy to all over Pakistan & International markets.Also open Sweet Shop in England.Sweet Shop name was named by his son.
